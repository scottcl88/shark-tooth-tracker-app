Help! My game is gone!
Tap on the Home tab in the bottom menu to view all teeth collected. Make sure you are logged in by going to 'Profile' and click 'Sign In' at the bottom. You may also need to restart your phone. If you are still missing data, contact support for further assistance at support@sharktoothtracker.com.
 How do I delete a tooth?
Touch the tooth on the home page and swipe up and then tap 'Delete'.
